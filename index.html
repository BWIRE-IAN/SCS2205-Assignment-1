The Green cross in Health.

The green cross is a widely recognized symbol used to indicate health, safety, and medical assistance. The green cross is a general marker often seen on pharmacies, first aid stations, and healthcare facilities. Its green color represents life, healing, and safety, making it a universal sign for places where medical help or supplies can be found.
